A Journey into the Network

This is a communal experiment, in which we attempt to harness the potential of a networked communion to enhance a shamanic practice.

Created by Karin Vališová and Jakub Fiala.

Let's set up the sound first

The experiment uses the signal from your computer's microphone to interact with the network.

We take privacy seriously — the audio itself never leaves your computer. The app merely detects when a loud sound occurs.

The red circle indicator below will blink when a sound is detected. Try moving the "Detection" slider, adjusting it so that the indicator only blinks when you speak, or make a noise with your rattle.

Instructions

In this experiment, our objective is to journey in non-ordinary reality, enter the networked space that connects all participants, and perceive what is there.

You will hear the processes within the network manifested in a sonic accompaniment, based on traditional shamanic drumming. If possible, take a rattle or a drum, and play along. The sounds you create will be detected, and will have an impact on what's going on inside of the network.

The experiment is divided into three equally long parts, denoted by three strokes of a bell. You do not need to react to these changes, but be mindful of the new section when it begins.

At the end, you shall hear the traditional callback signal.
